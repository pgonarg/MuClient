MU glTF Explorer

Click Open folder… and choose Data/ (or any subfolder).
Every .glb is listed on the left. Click one to view it in 3D.
Textures (sibling .png) load automatically.
Drag to orbit · scroll to zoom · ↑/↓ to step through models.
Needs internet (Three.js via CDN). Chrome/Edge/Firefox.
